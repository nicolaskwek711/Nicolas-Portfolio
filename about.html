who i am

about

hello, i am Nicolas! i am a 18 year-old, self-taught photographer and graphic designer currently studying a course in Story & Content Creation.

what initially started out as a small hobby a year back eventually turned into one of my favorite pastimes, and i have developed a strong passion for capturing small moments and telling stories through photos.

i have always enjoyed looking at photos, and am especially happy when a nice photo of me with my friends is taken. through my lens, i aim to deliver that same happiness to whomever i take photos for.

i am always open to new experiences and challenges, so if you are looking for an enthusiastic, friendly photographer, do get in touch and let's work together!

always creating.
